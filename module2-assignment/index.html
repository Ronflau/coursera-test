🔥🔥🔥 Spicy Vodka Rigatoni

Boil SALTY WATER and add RIGATONI until al denta. Pan fry CHOPPED TOMATOS, CHOPPED GARLIC and CHOPPED ONIONS with OLIVE OIL. Stir in CHILLI SAUCE, TOMATO PUREE and VODKA. Add CREAM and cook till hot(or skip this stage and use COOLED SALTY PASTA WATER with GRATED CHEESE at the end). Mix sauce with PASTA and add FRESH BASIL and CHEESE to your liking. Serve 🍽

🧀 Spaghetti alla Carbonara Romana 🧀

Boil SALTY WATER and add SPAGHETTI until al denta. Pan fry SALTED and PEPPERED CHOPPED PORK BELLY (best with GUANCIALE) until golden. Beat EGG YOLKS with COOKED PORK FAT. Take some SALTY PASTA WATER and let it cool until it stops steaming. Add COOLED PASTA WATER and GRATED ROMANO CHEESE into EGG mixture and mix until creamy texture. Stir in COOKED PASTA and CRUSHED BLACK PEPPER. Serve 🍽

🌿 Fresh Pesto Fusilli 🌿

Boil SALTY WATER and add FUSILLI until al denta. Blend NUTS OF YOUR LIKING, RAW GARLIC, FRESH BASIL LEAVES in OLIVE OIL to make a paste. Take some SALTY PSATA WATER and let it cool until it stops steaming. Mix COOLED PASTA WATER and CHEESE to make creamy sauce. Mix everything together. Serve 🍽
